So, we can write the first equation:

x + 4 = 10

We know that John already had 4 chocolates before Valentine's Day.

So, we can write the second equation:

x + 4 = 4

Now, we can solve the system of equations by subtracting the second equation from the first equation:

x + 4 - 4 = 10 - 4

This simplifies to:

x = 6

So, John was given 6 chocolates on Valentine's Day.
