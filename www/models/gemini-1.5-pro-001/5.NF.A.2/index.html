Problem: In their game, Kyle threw a ball for a distance of 18/4 yards, while Lisa threw the same ball for a distance of 19/9 yards. How many yards did the ball travel in total?

Response: 

Reasoning:  First find the sum of the two fractions: 18/4 + 19/9
The two fractions have unlike denominators so we must find a common denominator. The least common denominator for 4 and 9 is 36.
Multiply the numerator and denominator of the first fraction 18/4 by 9/9 to get (18*9)/(4*9) = 162/36.
Multiply the numerator and denominator of the second fraction 19/9 by 4/4 to get (19*4)/(9*4) = 76/36.
Now we can add the two fractions: 162/36 + 76/36 = 238/36
Simplify the fraction by dividing the numerator and denominator by their greatest common factor, 2: 238/2 = 119 and 36/2 = 18, so 238/36 = 119/18
This is an improper fraction, so convert to a mixed number: 119/18 = 6 11/18
