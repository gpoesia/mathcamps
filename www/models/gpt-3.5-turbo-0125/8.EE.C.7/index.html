Problem: Solve the following equation for the variable u: 122 = 92 + 28 * u

Response: 

To solve the equation 122 = 92 + 28u for u, we first subtract 92 from both sides to isolate the term with u:

122 - 92 = 92 + 28u - 92,
30 = 28u.

Next, we divide both sides by 28 to solve for u:

30/28 = (28u)/28,
u = 30/28.

Therefore, u = 15/14.
### Answer: u = 15/14.
