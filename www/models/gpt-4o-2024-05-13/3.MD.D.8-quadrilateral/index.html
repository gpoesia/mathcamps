Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Response: 

To find the width of the pool, we start by using the formula for the perimeter of a rectangle:

\[ P = 2L + 2W \]

where \( P \) is the perimeter, \( L \) is the length, and \( W \) is the width. We are given:

\[ P = 324 \, \text{meters} \]
\[ L = 83 \, \text{meters} \]

Substituting these values into the perimeter formula, we get:

\[ 324 = 2(83) + 2W \]

First, calculate \( 2 \times 83 \):

\[ 2 \times 83 = 166 \]

So the equation becomes:

\[ 324 = 166 + 2W \]

Next, isolate \( 2W \) by subtracting 166 from both sides:

\[ 324 - 166 = 2W \]
\[ 158 = 2W \]

Finally, solve for \( W \) by dividing both sides by 2:

\[ W = \frac{158}{2} \]
\[ W = 79 \]
