Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Response: 

Reasoning: The perimeter of a rectangle is given by the formula P = 2l + 2w, where l is the length and w is the width. We are given that the perimeter P is 324 meters and the length l is 83 meters. We can plug these values into the formula to get:

324 = 2(83) + 2w

Simplifying the equation, we get:

324 = 166 + 2w

Subtracting 166 from both sides, we get:

158 = 2w

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

79 = w

### Answer: 79
