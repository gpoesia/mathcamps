Problem: An equilateral triangle has a perimeter of 990cm. What is the length of each of its sides?

Response: 

Let's solve this step by step:

1) In an equilateral triangle, all sides have the same length. Let's call this length $x$.

2) The perimeter of a triangle is the sum of the lengths of all its sides. In this case, the perimeter is 990cm.

3) So, we can write an equation:

   $x + x + x = 990$
   
   or simplify to:
   
   $3x = 990$

4) To find $x$, we divide both sides by 3:

   $x = 990 \div 3 = 330$

Therefore, each side of the equilateral triangle is 330cm long.
