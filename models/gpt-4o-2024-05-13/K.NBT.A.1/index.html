Problem: Sarah had 4 candies. After her friend gave her some more, she had a total of 10 candies. How many candies did her friend give her?

\[ \text{Original candies} + \text{Candies given by friend} = \text{Total candies} \]

\[ \text{Candies given by friend} = 10 - 4 \]
\[ \text{Candies given by friend} = 6 \]
