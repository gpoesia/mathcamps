Problem: In their game, Kyle threw a ball for a distance of 18/4 yards, while Lisa threw the same ball for a distance of 19/9 yards. How many yards did the ball travel in total?

Gemma 2B: 

To find the total distance the ball traveled, we need to add the distances thrown by Kyle and Lisa.

First, let's convert the fractions to a common denominator. The denominators are 4 and 9. The least common multiple (LCM) of 4 and 9 is 36.

Convert \( \frac{18}{4} \) to a fraction with a denominator of 36:
\[ \frac{18}{4} = \frac{18 \times 9}{4 \times 9} = \frac{162}{36} \]

Convert \( \frac{19}{9} \) to a fraction with a denominator of 36:
\[ \frac{19}{9} = \frac{19 \times 4}{9 \times 4} = \frac{76}{36} \]

Now, add the two fractions:
\[ \frac{162}{36} + \frac{76}{36} = \frac{162 + 76}{36} = \frac{238}{36} \]

Simplify the fraction \( \frac{238}{36} \):
\[ \frac{238 \div 2}{36 \div 2} = \frac{119}{18} \]

Thus, the total distance the ball traveled is \( \frac{119}{18} \) yards.

### Answer: \( \frac{119}{18} \) yards

Expected answer: 119/18
