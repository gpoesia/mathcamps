Problem: Apple Inc. has 100 new smartphones in their main store. They sold 78 of them on the first day. If they received an additional shipment of 41 new smartphones the next day, how many new smartphones does the store have now?

Gemma 2B: 

First, we need to determine how many smartphones were left after the first day. Apple Inc. started with 100 smartphones and sold 78 of them:

\[ 100 - 78 = 22 \]

So, there were 22 smartphones left after the first day.

Next, we add the additional shipment of 41 new smartphones received the next day:

\[ 22 + 41 = 63 \]

Therefore, the store now has 63 new smartphones.
