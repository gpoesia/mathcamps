Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Gemma 2B: 

Reasoning: The perimeter of a rectangle is the sum of all its sides. Since the perimeter is 324 meters, we can set up the equation:

2(l + w) = 324

where l is the length and w is the width.

We are given that the length is 83 meters, so we can substitute this value into the equation:

2(83 + w) = 324

Simplifying the equation, we get:

166 + 2w = 324

Subtracting 166 from both sides gives:

2w = 158

Dividing both sides by 2 gives:

w = 79

### Answer: 79
