Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Gemma 2B: 

 Reasoning: The perimeter of a rectangle is given by the formula P = 2(l + w), where P is the perimeter, l is the length, and w is the width.
Substituting the given values, we get 324 = 2(83 + w).
Simplifying, we get 324 = 166 + 2w.
Subtracting 166 from both sides, we get 158 = 2w.
Dividing both sides by 2, we get w = 79.
Therefore, the width of the pool is 79 meters.
### Answer: 79
