Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Gemma 2B: 

 Reasoning: The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is P = 2l + 2w, where P is the perimeter, l is the length, and w is the width. We are given P = 324 and l = 83.

Solving for w:
324 = 2(83) + 2w
324 = 166 + 2w
2(324 - 166) = 2w
158 = 2w
w = 158 / 2
w = 79
### Answer: 79
