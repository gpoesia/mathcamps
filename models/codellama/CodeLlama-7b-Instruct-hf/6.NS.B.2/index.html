Problem: There are 73841 cubic centimeters of air needed to inflate a large beach ball. If we fill the ball up at a rate of 41 cubic centimeters per second, how many seconds will it take to fully inflate the beach ball?

CodeLlama 7B: 

  To find the time it will take to fully inflate the beach ball, we need to divide the total volume of air needed by the rate at which the ball is being filled.

First, let's convert the volume of air needed from cubic centimeters to liters:

73841 cubic centimeters = 73841/1000 liters

Next, we can use the formula:

Time = Total volume / Filling rate

Plugging in the values we know, we get:

Time = 73841/1000 liters / 41 cubic centimeters/second

Time = 178.8 seconds

Therefore, it will take approximately 178.8 seconds to fully inflate the large beach ball.
