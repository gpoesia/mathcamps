Problem: John has 2 tomatoes. He found the same amount of tomatoes in his garden. Later, he picked 7 more from his neighbor's garden. How many tomatoes did John have now?

Gemma 2B: 

  John had 2 tomatoes to start with.
He found the same amount of tomatoes in his garden, so he had 2 + 2 = 4 tomatoes.
Later, he picked 7 more tomatoes from his neighbor's garden, so he had 4 + 7 = 11 tomatoes.
Therefore, John had 11 tomatoes in total.

Expected answer: 11
