Multiplying both sides by 99.2 kg:

99.2 kg = 62.1 kg + x kg

Subtracting 62.1 kg from both sides:

Therefore, the total weight of the two eaten watermelons is 36.1 kg + 36.1 kg = 72.2 kg.
