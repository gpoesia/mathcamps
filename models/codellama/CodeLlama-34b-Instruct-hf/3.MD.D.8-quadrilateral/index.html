Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Response: 

  To find the width of the pool, we can use the formula for the perimeter of a rectangle, which is:

Perimeter = 2(l + w)

where l is the length and w is the width.

We are given that the perimeter is 324 meters and the length is 83 meters, so we can substitute these values into the formula to get:

324 = 2(83 + w)

Simplifying this equation, we get:

324 = 166 + 2w

Subtracting 166 from both sides gives us:

158 = 2w

Dividing both sides by 2 gives us:

79 = w

Therefore, the width of the pool is 79 meters.

Expected answer: 79
