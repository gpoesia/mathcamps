14^2 = x^2 + x^2

where x is the vertical height of the tent.

Simplifying the equation, we get:

196 = 2x^2

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

98 = x^2

Taking the square root of both sides, we get:

x = 10

So, the vertical height of the tent is 10 feet.
