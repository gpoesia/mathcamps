CodeLlama 34B on 3.MD.D.8-triangle

Common Core Standard 3.MD.D.8-triangle

This standard covers the following:

Solve real world and mathematical problems involving perimeters of polygons, including finding the perimeter given the side lengths, finding an unknown side length, and exhibiting rectangles with the same perimeter and different areas or with the same area and different perimeters.

Problem: An equilateral triangle has a perimeter of 990cm. What is the length of each of its sides?

CodeLlama 34B: 

  The perimeter of an equilateral triangle is equal to the sum of the lengths of its sides. Since the perimeter of this triangle is 990 cm, we can set up the equation:

s + s + s = 990

where s is the length of each side.

Solving for s, we get:

s = 990 / 3

s = 330

So, the length of each side of the equilateral triangle is 330 cm.

Expected answer: 330

Correct? Yes
