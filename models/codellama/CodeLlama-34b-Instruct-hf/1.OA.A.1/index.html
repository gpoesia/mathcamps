Problem: In a school competition, Aliyah correctly guesses 11 letter games and then correctly guesses 7 more games. But when she guesses the next game, she loses 6 times. What is the total number of letter games Aliyah correctly guessed in the end?

Gemma 2B: 

  Let's start by identifying the information given in the problem:

1. Aliyah correctly guesses 11 letter games.
2. She correctly guesses 7 more games after that.
3. She loses 6 times.

We can start by adding the number of games she correctly guessed in the first two steps:

11 + 7 = 18

Now, we need to subtract the number of times she lost:

18 - 6 = 12

So, the total number of letter games Aliyah correctly guessed in the end is 12.

Expected answer: 12

Correct? Yes
