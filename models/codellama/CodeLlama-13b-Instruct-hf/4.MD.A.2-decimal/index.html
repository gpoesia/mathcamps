A = w(w + 22.6)

A = 2w^2 + 45.2w

A = 2(3)^2 + 45.2(3)

Simplifying this expression, we get:

A = 18 + 138.6

A = 156.6

Therefore, the total area of Eva's garden after the extension is 156.6 square meters.
