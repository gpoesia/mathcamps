Given:
- Length of the rectangle is 21m
- Width of the rectangle is 37m

Perimeter = 2 × (length + width)

Calculation:
Perimeter = 2 × (21m + 37m)
Perimeter = 2 × 58m

Therefore, the perimeter of the rectangle is 116m.
