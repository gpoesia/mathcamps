Problem: A pentagon has four of its side lengths as 22m, 14m, 93m, and 75m respectively. If its total perimeter is 234m, find the length of the fifth side.

DeepSeek 67B: 

Reasoning:
The total perimeter of a pentagon is the sum of the lengths of all its sides.
Let's denote the length of the fifth side as x.
The total perimeter of the pentagon is given as 234m.
So, the sum of the lengths of all its sides is 234m.
We know the lengths of four sides, so we can calculate the length of the fifth side by subtracting the sum of the lengths of the known sides from the total perimeter.

Solution:
The sum of the lengths of the known sides is 22m + 14m + 93m + 75m = 204m.
The length of the fifth side is 234m - 204m = 30m.
