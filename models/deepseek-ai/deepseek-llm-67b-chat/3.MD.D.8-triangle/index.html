Problem: An equilateral triangle has a perimeter of 990cm. What is the length of each of its sides?

DeepSeek 67B: 

Reasoning:
An equilateral triangle has three equal sides. To find the length of each side, we can divide the perimeter by the number of sides.

Solution:
The perimeter of the equilateral triangle is 990 cm.
Since an equilateral triangle has three equal sides, we can divide the perimeter by 3 to find the length of each side.

Length of each side = Perimeter / Number of sides
Length of each side = 990 cm / 3
Length of each side = 330 cm

Each side of the equilateral triangle is 330 cm long.

Expected answer: 330
