Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Gemma 2B: 

Reasoning:
The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is P = 2L + 2W, where P is the perimeter, L is the length, and W is the width.
We are given the perimeter (P = 324 meters) and the length (L = 83 meters). We need to find the width (W).

Solution:
We can rearrange the formula to solve for W: W = (P/2) - L
Substituting the given values, we get: W = (324/2) - 83
W = 162 - 83
W = 79

The width of the pool is 79 meters.
