Problem: John has 2 tomatoes. He found the same amount of tomatoes in his garden. Later, he picked 7 more from his neighbor's garden. How many tomatoes did John have now?

Gemma 2B: 

Reasoning: John had 2 tomatoes initially. He found the same amount of tomatoes in his garden, so he now has 2+2 = <<2+2=4>>4 tomatoes. He picked 7 more from his neighbor's garden, so he now has 4+7 = <<4+7=11>>11 tomatoes.
### Answer: 11
