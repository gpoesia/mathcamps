Problem: An equilateral triangle has a perimeter of 696 meters. What is the length of each side of this triangle?

DeepSeek Math 7B Base: 

 Reasoning: The perimeter of an equilateral triangle is the sum of the lengths of its three sides. Since the triangle is equilateral, all three sides have the same length.
Let x be the length of each side of the equilateral triangle.
The perimeter of the triangle is 696 meters, so we have:
x + x + x = 696
Simplifying, we get:
3x = 696
Dividing both sides by 3, we get:
x = 696/3
x = 232
Therefore, the length of each side of the equilateral triangle is 232 meters.
