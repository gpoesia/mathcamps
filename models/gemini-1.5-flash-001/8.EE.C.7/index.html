Gemma 2B: 

Here's how to solve the equation:

1. **Simplify the right side:**  
   126 = 21v + 76

2. **Subtract 76 from both sides:**
   126 - 76 = 21v + 76 - 76
   50 = 21v

   50 / 21 = 21v / 21
   v = 50/21
