Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Response: 

Reasoning:  The perimeter of a rectangle is calculated by the formula:  Perimeter = 2 * (length + width) We know the perimeter is 324 meters and the length is 83 meters. Let's plug these values into the formula and solve for the width:

324 = 2 * (83 + width)

324 = 166 + 2 * width

324 - 166 = 2 * width

158 = 2 * width

width = 158 / 2

width = 79 meters

### Answer: 79 meters
