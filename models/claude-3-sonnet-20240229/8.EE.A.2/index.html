Problem: A child builds a sandcastle in a sandbox. The sandbox has the shape of a cubic box with 512 cubic feet of sand. What is the length of each side of the sandbox in feet?

Claude-3 Sonnet: 

To solve this problem, we need to find the side length of a cube given its volume.

Given information:
- The sandbox has the shape of a cubic box.
- The volume of the sandbox is 512 cubic feet.

Let's denote the side length of the cube as x.

Since the volume of a cube is given by the formula: Volume = side length^3

We can write:
512 = x^3

Taking the cube root of both sides:
x = ∛512
x ≈ 8 feet

Therefore, the length of each side of the sandbox is approximately 8 feet.
