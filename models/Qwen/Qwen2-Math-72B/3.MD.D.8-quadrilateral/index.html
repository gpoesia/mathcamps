Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

Qwen2-Math 72B: 

 Reasoning: The perimeter of a rectangle is given by the formula P = 2(l + w), where l is the length and w is the width.
We know that the perimeter is 324 meters and the length is 83 meters.
So, we can write the equation as 324 = 2(83 + w).
Solving for w, we get w = (324 - 2*83)/2 = <<(324 - 2*83)/2=79>>79 meters.
### Answer: 79
