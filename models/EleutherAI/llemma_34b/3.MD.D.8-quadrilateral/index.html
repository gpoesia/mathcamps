Problem: The perimeter of a rectangular swimming pool is 324 meters. The length of the pool is 83 meters. What is the width of the pool?

LLemma 34B: 

 Reasoning: The perimeter of a rectangle is 2*length + 2*width.
324 = 2*83 + 2*width
324 = 166 + 2*width
324 - 166 = 2*width
158 = 2*width
158/2 = <<158/2=79>>79 = width
